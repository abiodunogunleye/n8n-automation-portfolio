# AI Lead Capture & Follow-Up System

## What It Does
Automatically captures leads from a form, logs them to Google Sheets,
sends a personalised AI-generated reply within 2 minutes, and follows
up automatically after 48 hours if no response.

## Problem It Solves
Businesses lose leads by responding too slowly. This automation
responds to every enquiry within 2 minutes — 24/7 including weekends.

## Workflow Steps
1. Lead submits enquiry form
2. Data logged to Google Sheets (Status: New)
3. Owner notified by email instantly
4. Gemini AI generates personalised reply
5. AI reply sent to lead automatically
6. Status updated to Contacted
7. 24-hour wait per lead
8. Follow-up email sent automatically
9. Status updated to Followed Up + timestamp

## Tools Used
- n8n (workflow automation)
- Google Gemini AI (personalised email generation)
- Gmail (email delivery)
- Google Sheets (lead tracking)
